Getting Started

Nightfall's development began on January 3, 2023 as my first-ever GUI project for Roblox, specifically for use in Lumber Tycoon 2.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our GUI.

Collection of Information: When you execute the Nightfall GUI code, we collect the Roblox place ID of where the code was executed, the Roblox username and display name of the user, the executor type they used, and the time they executed the code. We collect this information through a Discord webhook.

Use of Information: We use the collected information to improve the Nightfall GUI and to provide technical support to our users.

Disclosure of Information: We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Security: We take steps to ensure the security of your personal information, including the use of industry-standard measures such as obfuscation of the code using a reputable service provider.

Updates to the Nightfall GUI: We value transparency and will keep you informed of any material changes to our GUI. Before any such changes become effective, we will notify you via Discord announcement pings. We encourage you to stay up-to-date by periodically checking our Discord server for the latest information and GUI updates. We also want to hear from you and will hold reaction role votes to gather your feedback and suggestions on the changes we should make.
